Travelling from abroad

If you are coming from the UK then there are few things you might want to know of before you land in India.

1. Flights - The airport you want to land on is the CSI Airport in Mumbai or SVP airport in Ahmedabad, then travel by land or get domestic connection to Rajkot or Jamnagar from Mumbai. There are 3 main airlines - Air India, British Airways and Jet Airways - that operate direct flights from London Heathrow to Mumbai, apart from many other indirect flights.

2. Visas - You will need a tourist visa to enter India. If you are a British Passport holder you can get a visa online. Just follow this link for further instructions: Indian visa

3. Weather - The weather in India is likely to be sunny with a maximum temperature of 30 degree Celsius and a minimum of 20 degree Celsius.

4. Transport - As you must have noticed that the wedding location is in city itself, so transportation wouldn't be a big issue. We will arrange bus or other mean of transportation for the main wedding day. For any other day to go and from wedding venues to hotel let us know we will arrage something, if that is not possible, we will guide everyone to the best available modes of transport and the best ways to book it.

5. Currency - The currency in India is the Indian Rupee. You will be able to buy currency at the airport, if you haven't got the currency before travelling. The best website to compare exchange rates is www.xe.com If you don't want to go to any foreign currency supplier, we can give you as many Indian Rupees you need at the prevalent bank rate and you can pay me GBP here in the UK. You can also return any unused Indian Rupees before you fly back and we will exchange it for GBP.

6. Further Travel - If you plan to make any further travels in India by train or air, there are few websites which might be helpful for you.

By Air - MakeMyTrip
By Rail - Indian rail

Apart from these, if you need any more information please feel free to contact us.
