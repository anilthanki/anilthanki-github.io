Anil Thanki

Norwich, United Kingdom thanki.anil@gmail.com

Anil is a Postdoctoral Research Scientist at the Earlham Institute before he worked as a Scientific Programmer at the Earlham Institute since 2011.

He has PhD in Bioinformatics from University of East Anglia, UK, Master of Science in Bioinformatics from the University of Leicester, UK and Bachelor of Science in Bioinformatics from Saurashtra University, India.

His area of interest is Bioinformatics tools development, with an interest in Open Science. He is also interested in web-based Data Visualisation, especially using D3.js.
